Everything Your Team Needs

Vantage is built for the way modern teams actually work — fast, collaborative, and context-rich.

Visual Project Boards

Create visual task cards, organize them in columns, and group them by department or priority. Keep your workspace structured and intuitive for everyone on the team.

Agentic AI Co-Pilot

Assign tasks to the AI Agent. It plans, calls web research tools, drafts documents, and proposes changes — all visible in a live execution trace. Think of it as a junior team member that never sleeps.

Human-in-the-Loop Review

Every AI proposal lands in a diff-review panel. Accept, modify, or reject changes before they touch your board. You stay in control — the AI amplifies, not replaces.

Real-Time Board Sync

Powered by Supabase Realtime. When a teammate drags a card or checks off a task, every connected browser updates instantly. Zero lag, zero refresh.

Heritage Timeline

Every project change, task completion, and key milestone is permanently recorded. New team members onboard in minutes, not weeks — your team's project history is fully preserved.

Secure Token Invites

Invite teammates with cryptographic one-time tokens tied to their email. No link-sharing abuse, no unauthorized access. Role-based permissions (Owner, Contributor, Reader) baked in.

Security & Sovereignty

Bring Your Own Database & Keys

We believe in absolute data ownership. Run Vantage on our secure shared cloud, or shift everything to your own infrastructure with two clicks.

BYODB

Bring Your Own Database

Connect Vantage directly to your private Supabase instance. By entering your own credentials in our advanced settings, you establish a direct, encrypted tunnel from your browser to your database.

  • Zero Vantage-side data storage or access
  • Custom Row-Level Security (RLS) controls
  • Complete PostgreSQL database sovereignty

BYOK

Bring Your Own Keys

Run agentic AI workflows on your own terms. Provide your Gemini or OpenAI API keys locally to fuel the AI co-pilot. Keep operations free-tier or scale up to advanced reasoning models at cost.

  • Keys remain encrypted in your browser's LocalStorage
  • Pay developers' raw API rates directly, no middleman markups
  • Offline simulator available for zero-cost local testing
