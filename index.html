Everything Your Startup Actually Needs

Not another generic Trello clone. Vantage is built for the way startups really work — fast, messy, context-heavy.

WhatsApp → Project Board

Paste your team's fragmented WhatsApp threads. AI extracts tasks, maps departments, builds checklists, and creates structured project cards — instantly. No more manual copy-paste.

Agentic AI Co-Pilot

Assign tasks to the AI Agent. It plans, calls web research tools, drafts documents, and proposes changes — all visible in a live execution trace. Think of it as a junior team member that never sleeps.

Human-in-the-Loop Review

Every AI proposal lands in a diff-review panel. Accept, modify, or reject changes before they touch your board. You stay in control — the AI amplifies, not replaces.

Real-Time Board Sync

Powered by Supabase Realtime. When a teammate drags a card or checks off a task, every connected browser updates instantly. Zero lag, zero refresh.

Heritage Timeline

Every supplier pivot, formulation change, and retail agreement is permanently recorded. New team members onboard in minutes, not weeks — your startup's DNA is preserved.

Secure Token Invites

Invite teammates with cryptographic one-time tokens tied to their email. No link-sharing abuse, no unauthorized access. Role-based permissions (Owner, Contributor, Reader) baked in.

From Chaos to Clarity in 5 Steps

Watch how Vantage transforms a disorganized group chat into a fully operational project board.

01

Paste Your Chat Logs

Copy-paste any WhatsApp, Slack, or Teams conversation into the importer. Upload a .txt export for bulk processing.

02

AI Parses & Structures

The AI identifies tasks, assigns departments, creates checklists, and groups related items into task bundles. You preview everything in a card deck before importing.

03

Manage on the Kanban Board

Drag cards across Backlog → Active → Review → Done. Filter by department, priority, or search. Every move is tracked in the heritage feed.

04

Deploy AI Agent

Assign any card to the AI Agent. It runs a full agentic loop: planning → tool calls (web research, doc drafting) → self-verification → proposal submission. Watch it think in real-time.

05

Review, Approve & Sync

Inspect the AI's proposals in a visual diff panel. One click to approve and merge — changes sync to all connected browsers instantly via Supabase Realtime.
